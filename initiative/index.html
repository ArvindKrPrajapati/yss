Our Initiatives

This thinking & feeling that we made someone’s life little better gives a great satisfaction. We collected urban waste to utilise it for many with dignity & also generated livelihood for many from this waste. We put a step forward with Aadharshila Basic Courses (ABC) for women to learn & earn their livelihood named as ” Roji Roti” scheme & also Aadharshila Bachpan Clubs (ABC) for little angels to make their life better. It bridges the existing learning gap of students of class 3rd, 4th and 5th.

About Us

Yash Sewa Samiti is an Indian non governmental organisation (NGO).It believes in selfless service of society to spread smiles for the upliftment & empowerment of the neglected and underprivileged sections of the society. We work for the various issues prevailing in our society such as Environmental issues, Menstrual Hygiene's , education and health for all.
